What we actually do.

Concrete operations, not vibes. Here's the work.

Takedowns & enforcement

We file and manage platform takedown requests (DMCA and platform-native claims), track them to resolution, and re-file when infringers come back. You approve; we execute.

Distribution operations

We handle the logistics of getting releases and content where they need to go, and keep listings, metadata, and links consistent across services.

Rights & catalog cleanup

We organize your ownership: splits, credits, release metadata, and a clean record of what you own so disputes are faster to win.

Monitoring & detection

We watch for re-uploads, impersonation, and stolen content, and flag it before it spreads instead of after.

Platform dispute handling

When a claim hits your content by mistake, we manage the counter-process so you're not stuck in a support queue.

Reporting

You get a plain-language record of what we found, what we filed, and where it stands — no dashboards you have to babysit.

Straight answers.

Are you a law firm?

No. Texas Movement Distribution is an operations service. We manage takedown, distribution, and monitoring processes. We do not provide legal advice or representation. For legal questions, talk to a licensed attorney.

What can you actually get removed?

Content that infringes rights you can document — stolen audio, re-uploaded video, impersonation, unauthorized reposts. We work through each platform's legitimate process. We don't do anything shady, and we don't promise removals we can't earn.

Do you guarantee results?

No, and be skeptical of anyone who does. Platforms make final calls. We commit to running the process correctly, persistently, and on time — including re-filing when infringers come back.
