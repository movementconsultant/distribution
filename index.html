How It Works

  1. Submit Your Case. Share links to the infringing content and your original work through our intake form.
  2. We Verify & Strategize. We review the evidence and choose the right notice type—DMCA, brand report, or platform-specific.
  3. We File & Follow Up. We submit takedown notices and chase responses until the content is removed.
  4. You Get Results. You receive a clear report with outcomes and recommended next moves.

FAQ

Which platforms do you support?

We primarily work across YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, Spotify, and major streaming and social platforms. If your issue is elsewhere, we’ll tell you honestly whether we can help.

Do you only use DMCA takedowns?

No. We use a mix of DMCA notices, brand impersonation reports, and platform-specific mechanisms depending on the situation.

How fast can content be removed?

Timelines depend on the platform and the case. We focus on clear communication and pushing each case as fast as policies allow.

Are you a law firm?

No. We are an operations and documentation partner for creators and brands. We do not provide legal advice. When a matter needs counsel, we help you organize the record for your attorney.
