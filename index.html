HOW IT WORKS

Rights-first, documented, platform-native, and calm.

Distribution work follows four principles so enforcement and catalog operations stay disciplined and legible.

Principle
Rights first.

We start from ownership—what’s yours, how it’s documented, and where it shows up—before we touch listings or enforcement.

Principle
Documented chain.

Maintain a clear chain of records: assets, releases, licenses, and actions. The record has to hold up when questioned.

Principle
Platform-native ops.

Operate in the language and rules of each platform—marketplaces, streaming, storefronts—rather than forcing one generic approach.

Principle
Enforce calmly.

Enforcement is evidence-based and repeatable, not reactive. We run workflows, not emotional responses.
